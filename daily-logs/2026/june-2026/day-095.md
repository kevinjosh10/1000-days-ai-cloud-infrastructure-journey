### 9️⃣ Learned About Cache Hits

```text
 ↓
```

### 🔟 Learned About Cache Misses

Studied cache retrieval failures.

Understood:

- Object is cached for future requests

```text

 ↓

 ↓
```

Learned:

- Cache misses occur on first requests or expired objects
