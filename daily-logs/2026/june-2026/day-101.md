### 1️⃣ Learned About Aurora Architecture

Studied how Aurora separates compute from storage.

Understood:
- Storage is distributed across 3 Availability Zones
- 6 copies of data are maintained
- 2 copies exist in each AZ
- The architecture is self-healing and fault tolerant

Visualized:

```text
Aurora Instance
      |
Shared Aurora Storage
      |
---------------------------------
| AZ1 | AZ1 | AZ2 | AZ2 | AZ3 | AZ3 |
---------------------------------
```

Learned:
- Aurora is designed for durability and resilience from the ground up
